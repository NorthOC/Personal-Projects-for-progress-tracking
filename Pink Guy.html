Pink Guy

The embodiment of everything a person should not be

The timeline of Pink Guy's music:

  • 2011 - George Miller created a YouTube channel called FilthyFrankTV where Pink Guy was born.
  • 2014 - George Miller releases an album under the name "PINK GUY".
  • 2016 - "Pink Season" was hinted throughout the videos on FilthyFrankTV until it was finally released.
  • 2017 - A remix EP titled Pink Season: The Prophecy was released featuring remixes by Borgore, Getter, Tasty Treat and Axel Boy.
  • 2017 - At the end of the year George Miller retires, ending the Pink Guy saga.
